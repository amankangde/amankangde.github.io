ℹ️ About the Game

Alien_Ghost 👽🚀 is a fun and challenging 2D mobile game inspired by the classic Flappy Bird, but with a unique twist! Instead of a bird, players control an alien sitting atop a UFO 🛸, navigating through obstacles by tapping the screen. With its simple yet engaging mechanics, Alien_Ghost tests the player's reflexes and ability to balance the UFO while adding a fresh sci-fi vibe.
This solo project was my opportunity to explore the fundamentals of mobile game development while adding my creative touch to an iconic gameplay style.


🎮 Features

• Tap-to-Jump Controls 📱: Easy-to-learn mechanics where tapping the screen propels the alien’s UFO upward.

• Dynamic Gameplay ⚡: Balancing the UFO while avoiding obstacles keeps the player on their toes.

• Procedural Obstacle Generation 🌀: Obstacles are dynamically instantiated for a fresh challenge every time.

• Unique Theme 🌌: A sci-fi spin featuring a quirky alien on a UFO.

• Minimalistic Visuals 🎨: Polished, clean 2D art style for an immersive experience.

• Replay Value 🔄: Simple yet addictive mechanics keep players coming back for more.


✅ My Contributions

• Designed and developed the entire game solo 🛠️, from initial concept to final build.

• Optimized performance to ensure smooth gameplay on Android devices.

• Implemented procedural instantiation for dynamically generating obstacles.

• Created intuitive, mobile-friendly controls 📱 for seamless player interaction.

• Designed the alien and UFO graphics 🛸 with a clean, 2D art style.

• Iterated on gameplay mechanics to add challenge and fun based on feedback.


🌟 What I Learned

This project gave me invaluable experience and introduced me to some essential aspects of game development:


• Automated Procedural Systems 🌀: Learned how to create dynamic, procedurally generated obstacles for endless gameplay.

• Game Optimization 🚀: Mastered techniques for optimizing 2D games for smooth performance on mobile devices.

• Adding Fun and Challenge 🎯: Explored balancing gameplay difficulty to create a fun and addictive player experience.

• Creative Problem-Solving 🧠: Gained experience in refining mechanics and visuals to match my creative vision.

🌟 Why It’s Special

- Alien_Ghost represents my ability to take a classic concept and make it uniquely my own. It allowed me to experiment with procedural systems and develop a deeper understanding of balancing fun and challenge. This game solidified my confidence in creating polished, mobile-friendly experiences and gave me a glimpse of the endless possibilities in game development.
