ℹ️ About the Game

Echoes of Evolution 🌿🧬 is a 2.5D side-scrolling platformer game created during the NostraGameJam 🏆, where our team proudly secured the Runner-up position 🥈! The game, inspired by the jam's theme of evolution, features a thrilling story about a boy who awakens after millions of years to a world where creatures have evolved, acid rain threatens survival, and evolution is the only path to progress.

As the protagonist, players must navigate a hostile environment, defeat evolved monsters 🦖🪰, collect evolution potions 🌟, and adapt to the new world to survive and advance.


🎮 Features

🔹 Bow and Arrow Combat 🏹: A unique mechanic I developed, complete with a visual prediction line for arrow trajectory, allowing players to aim and release with precision.

🔹 Evolved Enemies 🦖🪰: Multiple creature types with AI-driven behaviors and states that react dynamically to the player's proximity.

- Crawling Creatures 🐛: Ground-based monsters that require precise aim.

- Flying Monsters 🦅: Aerial threats that test timing and accuracy.


🔹 Acid Rain 🌧️: Environmental hazards that force players to find safe zones and adapt.

🔹 Evolution Potion 🌟: Collect potions to evolve and gain abilities that match the environment’s challenges.

🔹 Cinemachine Virtual Cameras 🎥: Seamless camera transitions for an immersive experience

🔹 Visually Stunning Environment 🌌: Detailed flora and fauna designed for a captivating experience, with 3D modeling and textured assets.

🔹 Team Collaboration 🤝: Improved communication, delegation, and coordination with teammates under pressure.


✅ My Contributions

As the Lead Developer, I took charge of the following:

🔹 Core Mechanics Development 🏹: Designed and implemented the bow and arrow system from scratch, including the visual trajectory line for aiming.

🔹 Enemy AI and Behavior Design 🤖: Created multiple states for enemies to dynamically react based on player proximity (near/far).

🔹 Player Mechanics 🎮: Built smooth and intuitive controls for an engaging player experience.

🔹 HUD and UI Design 🖥️: Designed the user interface, health bars, and progression indicators for better player engagement.

🔹 Environment Design 🌿: Modeled and textured plants, rocks, and other assets to create a lush, immersive world.

🔹 Sound Implementation 🎵: Added sound effects and background music to enhance the atmosphere.

🔹 Optimization 🚀: Ensured the game performed well on both Android and Web platforms.

🔹 Team Management 🤝: Collaborated effectively with my team to ensure deadlines were met and the game aligned with the jam's theme and vision.
